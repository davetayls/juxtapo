Enhance your productivity with plugins?

library Juxtapo already has a collection of plugins which will enhance your development. Some of these are bundled in with the download.

If you have developed a plugin for juxtapo then email it to us and we will feature it here on the site.

Bundled Plugins

The following plugins are bundled in with the download

  • QUnit: Allows for in build unit testing using the qunit library
  • Google Spreadsheets: Get your design templates and template metadata from a Google spreadsheet
  • Design Info: Show the positioning and size of the current design
  • Views: Different displays for the template thumbnails
